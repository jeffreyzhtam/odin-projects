Mango Lassi

Decription

Mango Lassi is a popular lassi variety made with Indian yogurt, ripe sweet mangoes and cardamoms

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Cups Chopped Mangoes
  • 1 Cup Yogurt
  • 1/2 Cup Milk
  • 2 Tablespoons Sugar
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Cardamom Powder

Directions

  1. Peel, chop and freeze mangoes until really cold
  2. Add mangoes, sugar, cardamom powder and milk to a blender jar
  3. Blend until really smooth and then add yogurt
  4. Blend again until very smooth, avoid over blending as it can sometimes become slimy
